Gary Houck

Web Designer and Developer.

My name is Gary and I’m a designer based out of the twin cities of Minnesota. Creative exploration has been my lifelong passion with my journey beginning as a musician. I left High School at 16 to complete a music degree and in my early 20’s I played guitar for a band who reached number 15 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. My interests eventually shifted to film-making, photography and other visual mediums which have refueled my passion for art.

In my spare time you can find me either watching The Office over and over, playing around with Photoshop, or pretending to know what I’m doing while raising three kids as a step-dad.

My professional skills are diverse and include print design, photography and editing, video and audio production, web design, and some pretty sweet guitar skills. I am well versed in Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Premier Pro, InDesign, and several brands of audio editing software. Please contact me with any comments, questions, or ideas how I can help you. Let’s make something cool together!
