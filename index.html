My name is Gary and I’m a designer and web developer based out of the twin cities of Minnesota. Creative exploration has been my lifelong passion with my journey beginning as a musician. I left High School at 16 to complete a music degree and in my early 20’s I played guitar for a band who reached number 15 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. My interests eventually shifted to film-making, photography and other visual mediums which have refueled my passion for creative energy.

The following work below includes a mix of professional, personal, and school projects that showcase my design and coding skills. Please contact me with any comments, questions, or ideas how I can help you.
